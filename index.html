InterviewMentor explained.

InterviewMentor is a AI powered web application that helps users prepare for their upcoming interviews. Users can practice their interview skills by answering questions and receiving feedback on their answers along with a score out of 10.

Why I made InterviewMentor.

I made InterviewMentor because I wanted to help people prepare for their interviews. I know that interviews can be stressful and I wanted to make a tool that would help people feel more confident about their interviews.
