InterviewMentor explained.

InterviewMentor is a AI powered web application that helps users prepare for their upcoming interviews. Users can practice their interview skills by answering questions and receiving feedback on their answers along with a score out of 10.

Why I made InterviewMentor.

I made InterviewMentor while preparing for my co-op intake interview because I wanted to help people prepare for any sort of interview. I know that interviews can be stressful and I wanted to make a tool that would help people feel more confident about their interviews and alleviate their stress. This is why InterviewMentor mimics a real interview by adding a "virtual" human asking you questions. Feedback is given in real time after each question tailored toward each unique question.

I also know that you have specific questions you may want to test yourself on which is why InterviewMentor allows you to input any question you would like. By providing a powerful tool to practice and refine interview skills, I hope to help more people land their dream jobs.
